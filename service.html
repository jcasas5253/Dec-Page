Building a Culture of Excellence with PrAISe

In 2025, we introduced PrAISe, an inspiring recognition program that celebrates team members who go above and beyond in the way they care for our customers. Every interaction is a chance to build trust, solve problems, and create a positive experience - and PrAISe highlights the people who consistently deliver on that level of excellence.

Throughout the year, service team members demonstrated:

• Exceptional customer handling – listening with empathy, taking ownership, and following through

• Professionalism and teamwork – supporting colleagues and representing AIS and Mercury with integrity

• Commitment to quality – getting it right the first time, and finding ways to improve the customer experience

• Owning performance behaviors – taking accountability for key performance metrics (call volume, talk time, productivity, and more) and making thoughtful decisions to support them
