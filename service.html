AI Virtual Assistant Expansion:

Our AI Virtual Assistant, Nicole, is now taking auto policy change requests during business hours! As of March 7th, customers may choose to speak with Nicole during business hours after waiting on hold for over 2 minutes. Just like after hours, customers can add/remove drivers, add/remove/replace vehicles and update addresses.

Customer Experience Surveys:

We are pleased to announce that we are transitioning our sales and service Customer Experience Surveys from InMoment to Forsta. Forsta is a leading Voice of the Customer (VOC) platform, designed to improve our administrative processes and help us gain deeper insight into our customers.

With Forsta, we gain:

✅ Enhanced surveys - More streamlined, saving customers' time

✅ Advanced Insights - Deeper, more actionable customer data with PowerBI integration

✅ Automation - Faster, more efficient feedback collection and analysis

✅ User-Friendly Experience - An intuitive interface for leaders that makes it easier to navigate and use
