Starting 2025

We are off to another great start in 2025, finishing with 10,916 sales representing a 108% improvement over January 2024. Auto conversion in California is close to 50% which is a 130% increase over prior year, when we averaged 38%. Without a doubt, we are beginning to see a decline in consumer demand and for the first time in several months, we experienced a decrease in quote volume.

We are now beginning to automate fllow-up on all leads using AI to assist with moving online Customers to inbound phone calls to increase quote volume. We are also beginning to purchase leads in addition to increasing our spend in digital marketing, both inside and outside California.
